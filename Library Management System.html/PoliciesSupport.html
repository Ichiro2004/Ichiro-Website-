Library Policies and Support

Library Policies

  • Policy 1: All books must be returned within the specified time frame.
  • Policy 2: Late fees will be charged for overdue books.
  • Policy 3: Books should be handled carefully to avoid damage.
  • Policy 4: Only registered users are allowed to borrow books.
  • Policy 5: Any lost or damaged books must be reported immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I borrow a book?
    A: You can borrow books by registering as a student and issuing a book through the library system.
  • Q: What happens if a book is overdue?
    A: Late fees will be charged for every day the book is overdue. Please return it on time to avoid fines.
  • Q: Can I reserve a book that's checked out?
    A: Yes, you can reserve a book, and you'll be notified when it becomes available.
